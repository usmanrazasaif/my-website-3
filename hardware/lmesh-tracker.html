Product Overview


The Tracker Device is a battery-operated prototyping platform for cellular IoT, certified for global operation. It integrates the nRF9160 System-in-Package (SiP), supporting LTE-M, NB-IoT and GNSS, This platform offers an ideal solution for swiftly developing prototypes of various cellular IoT (cIoT) concepts, with a particular emphasis on asset tracking applications. It leverages the built-in GNSS of the nRF9160 SiP to accurately determine the device's position and utilizes the integrated accelerometers to perform motion analysis and activate sleep mode when there is no activity detected.
